How do I create my BulkMein Account?
It's easy, go to our login page (Click on New user/Signup) or click here, fill your basic credentials and submit. Congratulations! You are part of BulkMein gang now.
How do I place an order?
To place your order follow 3 simple steps mentioned below:
  1. Step 1: Add products to your cart
  2. Step 2: Go to checkout and add your delivery address
  3. Step 3: Review your order and make payment
What is the minimum order quantity?
Our minimum order quantity is 25kg, which can consist of one single product or multiple products with overall weight of 25kg or more.
  1. Minimum order quantity per product – 5 Kgs
  2. Minimum Buying Quantity – 25 kgs
How do I know if my order was placed successfully?
Sit back and relax. You’ll receive a confirmation message once your order is confirmed. You’ll receive another message once your order is shipped Your Invoice, Eway Bill, Tracking Details and other detail will also be available on your order page.
How do I cancel the order I have placed?
Tap on “My Orders” section under the main menu of your Website and then select the order you want to cancel. The 'Cancel' option will only be available before your order is shipped. If you are facing an issue, please email us at contact@bulkmein.com or call us on +911234567890 and we will sort it for you.
How do I check status of my order?
It's simple, go to my order section on your profile and select the order for which you want to know status.
How do I track my order?
You can track your order once it has been dispatched from our warehouse. An email/SMS notification will be sent with a tracking link. You can also track it from your account on the website by Selecting ‘Orders’ from the top right corner and then Downloading the tracking receipt. You can then track your consignment from our designated delivery partner’s portal.
How long will it take for my order to be delivered?
Once shipped, our products are typically delivered in 3-5 business days in metros, and 5-7 business days for the rest of India. Delivery time may vary depending upon the shipping address and other factors (public holidays, extreme weather conditions, etc.).
Can I modify the shipping address of my order before it has been shipped?
Yes, you can modify the shipping address before order is shipped. Call us at +911234567890 and our executive will assist you with it.
Are there any additional shipping charges?
Shipping charges are calculated based on order quantity and delivery address at the time of checkout. There are no hidden charged thereafter. In case if customer raises request to modify the shipping address the difference in shipping amount has to be paid by the customer.
What if my order is undelivered?
Our delivery partner will re-attempt the delivery. Still if our courier partners are unable to deliver the product and they send it back to us, we will initiate a refund to your source account within 2-3 working days.
What if my address is not serviceable?
Don’t worry. Reach us out at +911234567890 or write to us on contact@bulkmein.com +911234567890 and our executive will help you with alternate delivery options.
What should I do if my payment fails?
In case there is a failure in payment, please retry and keep the following things in mind:
  1. Please confirm if the information you’ve provided is correct i.e. account details, billing address, and password (for Net Banking); and that your internet connection wasn’t disrupted during the process.
  2. If your account has been debited even after a payment failure, it is normally rolled back to your bank account within 7-10 business days. For any further clarification, you can email us at contact@bulkmein.com or at 919999999. ( add new mail for payments)

When will I receive my refund?
Refund process is initiated once the return is complete. This usually takes 2-3 working days after the return material is received at our warehouse.

I have created a return request. When will the product be picked up?
Once we receive this request, someone from the courier partner's team will arrive at the address for a pickup within 2 business days. In case if pickup is unavailable for your location, you can self-ship the product to our address and we compensate your courier charges with the refund amount.
